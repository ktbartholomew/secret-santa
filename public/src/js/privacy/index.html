Privacy Policy

What We Collect

We collect your name, e-mail, and current profile picture from Facebook whenever you log in with Facebook. We collect any information you provide to Secret Santa directly, such as your likes or dislikes.

Who We Share it With

Your name, current profile picture, and information you provide to Secret Santa directly will be shared with others who have joined the same game as you. We don’t share any of your information with third parties.

Deleting Your Data

If you wish to have all your personal data removed from Secret Santa, ask Keith and he’ll do it, no questions asked.
